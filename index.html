Top Destinations & Highlights

Phu Quoc Island

Experience the paradise of Phu Quoc with stunning beaches, exciting tours, and the world’s longest sea-crossing cable car.

Top Tours

  • 3 Island VIP Tour - Snorkeling and BBQ on hidden beaches
  • Squid Fishing Night Tour - Catch squid under the stars
  • Hidden Gems Trip - Waterfalls, farms, and secret beaches
  • Starfish Beach & Safari - Visit Vinpearl Safari Park
  • Longest Cable Car to Hon Thom Island (7,899m) - Guinness World Record!
  • Scuba Diving & Coral Reef Exploration

Best Restaurants & Bars

  • Mango Bay Resort Restaurant
  • The Pepper Tree
  • Sailing Club Phu Quoc

Nightlife & Events

  • Full Moon Party at Sailing Club
  • Sunset Cruises with cocktails
  • New Year Gala at JW Marriott Phu Quoc

Hanoi

Explore the charming Old Quarter, cultural landmarks, street food and vibrant nightlife of Vietnam’s capital.

Top Tours

  • Old Quarter Walking Tour
  • Water Puppet Theater
  • Day Trip to Ninh Binh (Halong Bay on land)

Best Restaurants & Bars

  • Bún Chả Hương Liên (Obama’s favorite)
  • Ta Hien Street Beer Corner
  • The Rooftop Bar

Nightlife & Events

  • Tadioto Hidden Bar
  • 1900 Le Théâtre Club
  • Tet New Year Festival & Mid-Autumn Festival

Ha Long Bay & Caves

Cruise among limestone karsts, explore caves and hidden lagoons in this UNESCO World Heritage site.

Top Tours

  • Overnight Cruise on Ha Long Bay
  • Visit Sung Sot (Surprise) Cave
  • Ti Top Island for beach and views

Restaurants

  • Seafood at Bai Chay Market

Da Nang

Modern city with beaches, mountains, and vibrant nightlife near the historic Hoi An.

Top Tours

  • Marble Mountains hike
  • Ba Na Hills & Golden Bridge
  • Son Tra Peninsula Nature Tours

Best Restaurants & Bars

  • Heaven Bar
  • The Rachel Restaurant & Bar

Nightlife

  • Sky36 Club (Vietnam's tallest rooftop club)
  • Beach clubs & underground venues
