Top 10 Destinations in Vietnam

Discover the must-see destinations across Vietnam—from bustling cities to tranquil islands. We'll update this list regularly with new experiences and hidden gems to explore.

Hanoi

Vietnam's capital blends centuries-old architecture with vibrant street culture.

  • Explore Old Quarter
  • Visit Hoan Kiem Lake
  • Try Egg Coffee

Ha Long Bay

World-famous limestone islands and emerald waters—perfect for cruising and kayaking.

  • Cruise the Bay
  • Kayak between islands
  • Visit Caves

Hoi An

A UNESCO heritage town known for lantern-lit streets and traditional charm.

  • Lantern Festival
  • Tailor-made Clothes
  • Ancient Town Walk

Ho Chi Minh City

The bustling southern metropolis known for its vibrant nightlife and rich history.

  • Visit War Remnants Museum
  • Explore Ben Thanh Market
  • Enjoy Rooftop Bars

Da Nang

Modern city famous for its sandy beaches, Dragon Bridge, and proximity to heritage sites.

  • Walk Dragon Bridge at Night
  • Relax on My Khe Beach
  • Visit Marble Mountains

Nha Trang

Popular coastal city with beautiful beaches, water sports, and vibrant nightlife.

  • Snorkel and Dive
  • Relax on the Beach
  • Enjoy Night Markets

Sapa

Mountain town known for stunning terraced rice fields and ethnic minority cultures.

  • Trek the Rice Terraces
  • Visit Hill Tribe Villages
  • See Fansipan Mountain

Hue

Historic city famous for its Imperial Citadel and royal tombs.

  • Visit Imperial Citadel
  • Explore Royal Tombs
  • Try Hue Cuisine

Phu Quoc

Island paradise known for its pristine beaches, snorkeling, and fresh seafood.

  • Relax on Long Beach
  • Snorkel in An Thoi Islands
  • Visit Pepper Farms

Mekong Delta

The "Rice Bowl" of Vietnam with floating markets, lush greenery, and river life.

  • Visit Floating Markets
  • Boat Ride through Canals
  • Explore Fruit Orchards

Vietnam Travel Tips

  • Always carry cash—many places don't accept cards.
  • Try the local street food—it’s safe and delicious!
  • Use Grab or Be for cheap and reliable transport.
