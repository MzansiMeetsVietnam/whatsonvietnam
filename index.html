Vietnam Visa Information

Most visitors require a visa to enter Vietnam. Options include e-visa, visa on arrival, or embassy visas. Check the latest official info and apply online at the Vietnam Immigration E-visa Portal.

Visa exemptions: Citizens from several countries have visa exemptions for stays ranging from 14 to 90 days. Always verify current rules before travel.
